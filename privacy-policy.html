Privacy Policy

Last updated 30 June 2026

This policy describes how BillFlow ("we", "the app") collects and uses your data. By using BillFlow you agree to this policy.

1. What we collect

  • Account: email address and an encrypted refresh token, used to sign you in across devices.
  • Business profile: business name, address, GST and license numbers, phone — to print on your invoices.
  • Operational data: products, customers, suppliers, bills, payments, prescriptions metadata. Stored in your own per-user database on this device and synced to your account on Supabase.

2. What stays on your device

Prescription image files (photos and scans of doctors' prescriptions) are saved to your local disk only. The image bytes are never uploaded. Only the structured Schedule H1 register fields (patient name, doctor name, date, diagnosis) sync.

3. How your data is secured

  • Local data is held in SQLite per user (billflow_<userId>.sqlite). Each user on the same machine has an isolated store.
  • Refresh tokens are encrypted on Windows via DPAPI before being written to the registry.
  • All cloud calls go over HTTPS to Supabase.
  • Row-Level Security on the server limits every read and write to rows you own.

4. Subscriptions and payments

BillFlow uses Razorpay for subscription processing. Razorpay receives the minimum information required to complete the transaction (name, email, billing details). Subscription fees are non-refundable once the billing cycle has started.

5. Sharing

We do not sell your data. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — to host your account and synced data.
  • Razorpay — to process subscription payments.
  • Authorities — when legally required.

6. Your rights

You can sign out at any time, which clears the local session on this device. To delete your account and server-side data, contact us at the address below.
